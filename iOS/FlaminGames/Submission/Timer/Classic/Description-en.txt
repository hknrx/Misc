Here comes yet another game in the "Flamin" serie: Flamin Timer! No need to visit a mall, enjoy this classic arcade machine at any time and try to get as many coins as possible! A single and simple rule: attempt to stop the timer on 1000! Sounds easy, no?! Compare your score online with other players and try to unlock all the achievements!
