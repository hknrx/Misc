# Description #
###############

Evil mazes, a timer, your skills: welcome to Flamin Maze! Adapted from a famous arcade machine, you can now enjoy 2 game modes on your iPhone or iPod touch: Escape from as many levels as possible before the time runs out, drawing a path from the entrance to the farther spot of the labyrinth, or explore each level as quickly as possible by lighting up each single square - easy to understand, but hard to master! Go compare your performances with other players online and try unlock all the achievements... if you dare!

* You like Flamin Maze? Make sure to also have a look to Flamin Stack and Flamin Timer! *
