Welcome to Paper Globe-Trotter!

Do you feel like an artist today? Take a paper sheet and a few colored pencils to create your own little character, your "Paper Globe-Trotter"! Also draw your moving inn to welcome guests from all around the world, and follow your globe-trotter while he's travelling far away from home, visiting others' inns!
 • Receive post cards from your globe-trotter;
 • Look at your guests and give them an appreciation;
 • Check your globe-trotter history with his passport;
 • Check your inn's history (guest book);
 • Publish feeds and screenshots to Facebook!

Have fun with Paper Globe-Trotter!
